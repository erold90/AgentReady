What is WebMCP?

WebMCP is a new W3C standard (backed by Google & Microsoft) that lets websites expose structured actions to AI agents in the browser. Think of it as making your website "API-ready" for AI — without building an actual API.

With WebMCP, an AI agent can discover your site's forms, understand what they do, fill them with structured data, and receive structured results — all running locally in the user's browser.
